Effective Date: February 13, 2026

By installing, accessing, or using **Celia Clips** ("the Software"), you agree to the collection and use of data as described in these Data Usage Terms.

## 1. What Data We Collect

When you use the Software, we may collect the following anonymized technical and usage data ("Usage Data"):

*   **Clip Metadata**: Duration, visual style (e.g., split screen, full screen), and hook type of generated clips.
*   **Performance Metrics**: Predicted viral scores (virality score), retention estimates, and platform-specific metrics (if available).
*   **System Information**: Software version, operating system, and hardware capabilities (e.g., GPU availability).
*   **Errors and Diagnostics**: Logs related to crashes or processing failures.

**We DO NOT collect:**
*   Audio or video content of your episodes.
*   Transcripts or text content (unless explicitly opted-in for specific features).
*   Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, emails, or addresses, except as necessary for authentication (e.g., Google Login).

Inminente Podcast ("We") uses Usage Data for:
1.  **Product Improvement**: Optimizing algorithms for clip selection and virality prediction.
2.  **Collective Intelligence**: Aggregating data across users to identify trends in successful content formats.
3.  **Research & Reporting**: Generating anonymized industry reports on podcast trends.
4.  **Commercial Purposes**: We reserve the right to sell, license, or share **aggregated and anonymized** insights derived from Usage Data to third parties.

## 3. Data Processing & Privacy

*   **Anonymization**: All Usage Data is processed to remove identifiers linking it to specific content or individuals before aggregation.
*   **Storage**: Data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption.
*   **Pipelines**: Data ingestion occurs via secure, authenticated endpoints (e.g., Supabase Edge Functions).

*   **Consent**: By using the Software, you consent to this data collection.
*   **Opt-out**: You may request to opt-out of data collection by contacting us at [hola@inminente.co](mailto:hola@inminente.co).
